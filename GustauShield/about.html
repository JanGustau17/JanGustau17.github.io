Why This Website?

I built GustauShield to explore a crucial idea: in cybersecurity, the same skills and tools, including powerful AI, can be used for good or for harm.

Too often, terms like 'hacker' are only painted in a negative light. I want to show that many brilliant minds in this field—whether they call themselves hackers, analysts, or researchers—are dedicated to protecting us and building a safer digital future.

This site is my way of sharing what I'm learning, especially about how rapidly AI is changing the game for both attack and defense.

My hope is that these insights spark curiosity and encourage others, particularly the next generation, to think deeply about these technologies.

Ultimately, it's about understanding the complex 'Matrix' of our digital world. The more we understand, the better equipped we are to make informed choices and use our own talents for positive change and digital freedom.
